<h1>🌙 Otome Visual Novel Project</h1>

<h2>📖 Overview</h2>
<p>
  This is a <strong>story-driven otome visual novel</strong> set in a modern environment
  with hidden supernatural elements.
</p>

<p>
  The story begins when the protagonist encounters an injured stranger in the forest —
  a young man with wolf ears and no memory of who he is.
</p>

<p>
  From that moment on, her quiet life begins to change.
</p>

<hr />

<h2>🎮 Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Choice-based narrative</li>
  <li>Multiple endings</li>
  <li>Relationship-driven progression</li>
  <li>First-person perspective</li>
  <li>Strong inner monologue</li>
</ul>

<h2>🐺 Main Character — Lowei</h2>

<h3>Basic Info</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Name:</strong> Lowei</li>
  <li><strong>Race:</strong> Werewolf</li>
  <li><strong>Status:</strong> Amnesiac</li>
</ul>

<h3>Appearance</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Short messy white hair</li>
  <li>Golden eyes</li>
  <li>Wolf ears and tail matching his hair color</li>
  <li>Torn white T-shirt and ripped jeans</li>
  <li>Visible scratches and injuries</li>
</ul>

<h3>Personality</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Quiet and polite</li>
  <li>Slightly awkward</li>
  <li>Observant and cautious</li>
  <li>Strong protective instinct</li>
  <li>Emotionally restrained</li>
</ul>

<h2>👩‍💻 Protagonist</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Independent programmer</li>
  <li>Lives alone in a quiet suburban house</li>
  <li>Rational and calm</li>
  <li>Kind, but not naive</li>
</ul>

<h2>🔀 Gameplay</h2>
<p>The story progresses through player choices that affect:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Character trust</li>
  <li>Relationship development</li>
  <li>Story direction</li>
</ul>

<h3>Endings</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Happy Ending:</strong> Truth uncovered</li>
  <li><strong>Normal Ending:</strong> Partial resolution</li>
  <li><strong>Bad Ending:</strong> Trust collapses</li>
</ul>

<h2>🎭 Narrative Style</h2>
<ul>
  <li>First-person storytelling</li>
  <li>Dialogue-focused writing</li>
  <li>Realistic tone with supernatural tension</li>
  <li>Slow-burn relationship development</li>
</ul>
